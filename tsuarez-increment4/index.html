Welcome to MonoMuse

MonoMuse is a contemporary museum dedicated to exploring the intersection of art, technology, and human creativity. Through immersive exhibitions and interactive installations, MonoMuse invites visitors to discover how digital innovation is transforming the way we create and experience art.

Whether you are a first-time visitor or a returning member, our exhibitions offer new perspectives on the role of technology in culture and everyday life. Plan your visit to experience cutting-edge exhibitions, hands-on experiences, and events designed to inspire curiosity and creativity. Our rotating collections feature work from both emerging and established artists working at the intersection of digital media, sculpture, and performance. MonoMuse is open year-round, offering guided tours, artist talks, and family programming every month.
